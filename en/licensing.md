# License and activation

Skribane includes a free 21-day trial with all features unlocked. When the trial period ends, you need an active subscription to continue using the application.

## Free trial

When you install Skribane for the first time, a **21-day** trial period starts automatically. During this time you can use all features without limitations: writing, AI, Codex, export, backup — everything.

You don't need to sign up or enter payment details to start the trial.

### What happens when the trial ends?

An activation screen appears asking you to enter a license key. Until you do, you can't access the editor or the rest of the app.

::: tip Your data is safe
Your data is **never deleted** when the trial expires. Your novel, your Codex and all your configuration remain intact. You can also **always export your manuscript** (DOCX or PDF) from the activation screen, even without a license.
:::

### Trial badge

While you're on the trial, you'll see a badge in the top bar showing the remaining days (e.g., "Trial · 15 days left"). When 3 days or less remain, the badge turns red to warn you.

## Subscribe to Skribane

Skribane works on a **subscription** model: **€11.99/month** or **€9.99/month** billed annually (€119.88/year). While your subscription is active, you receive all updates: new features, bug fixes and security improvements.

To subscribe:

1. Visit [www.skribane.com](https://www.skribane.com) and click **Subscribe**.
2. Choose your plan (monthly or annual) and complete the payment. Payments are handled through Lemon Squeezy, which acts as the merchant of record and handles global taxes and invoicing.
3. You'll receive a **license key** by email (format: `XXXXXXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXXXXXXXXXX`).

## Updates

While your subscription is active, you receive **all updates** at no additional cost.

### What happens if I cancel my subscription?

When you cancel, you keep access until the end of your paid period. After that, a lock screen is displayed, but:

- You can **always export your manuscript** (DOCX or PDF) from the lock screen.
- **Your data is never deleted.** Your novel, your Codex and all your configuration remain intact.
- AI features are unavailable while the subscription is inactive.

To use Skribane again, simply reactivate your subscription at [www.skribane.com](https://www.skribane.com).

## Activate the license

You can activate your license in two ways:

### From the activation screen (expired trial)

If the trial has ended, the activation screen appears automatically. Enter your license key and click **Activate**.

### From Configuration (during trial or after)

1. Go to **Config** in the sidebar.
2. Find the **License** section (at the bottom).
3. Enter your license key and click **Activate**.

Activation requires an internet connection (it validates against the Lemon Squeezy server). Once activated, Skribane works offline with a local cache that revalidates every 7 days.

## Activation on multiple computers

Your license allows **up to 2 simultaneous activations**. This lets you use Skribane on two different computers (e.g., your desktop and your laptop).

### What if I reach the 2-activation limit?

If you try to activate on a third computer, you'll get an error indicating you've reached the limit. To fix this, **deactivate** the license on one of the previous computers:

1. Open Skribane on the computer you no longer need.
2. Go to **Config** → **License** section.
3. Click **Deactivate license** and confirm.

This frees up an activation so you can use it on the new computer.

### What if I no longer have access to the previous computer?

If your computer has broken down, been reformatted or you no longer have access, write to **contacto@skribane.com** with your license key. We can manually deactivate the instance.

## Deactivate the license

You can deactivate your license at any time from **Config** → **License** → **Deactivate license**. This frees up an activation so you can use it on another computer.

::: warning
Deactivating the license does not delete your data. Your novel and your Codex remain on the computer. Access is simply locked until you reactivate the license or enter a different one.
:::

## Offline usage

Skribane validates the license online when you first activate it. After that, it works with a **7-day local cache**. This means you can use Skribane without internet for a week. If more than 7 days pass without a connection, the app will ask to revalidate the license when you reconnect.

## License FAQ

### How does the subscription work?

Skribane works on a subscription: €11.99/month or €9.99/month billed annually (€119.88/year). While your subscription is active, you receive all updates.

### What happens if I cancel my subscription?

You keep access until the end of your paid period. After that, a lock screen is displayed. You can always export your manuscript and your data is never deleted.

### Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled through Lemon Squeezy according to their terms of service. You can contact contacto@skribane.com if you have any issues.

### Can I switch computers?

Yes. Deactivate the license on the old computer and activate it on the new one. You have 2 simultaneous activations.

### What if I reinstall Windows?

Your license remains valid. Install Skribane again and enter your license key. If you had used both activations, the reinstall may count as a new activation — deactivate the previous one if needed, or contact us.
