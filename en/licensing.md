# License and download

Skribane is **free**. Download it from [www.skribane.com](https://www.skribane.com) — no sign-up, no credit card required.

There is no trial, no activation, no limits. All features are available from the very first moment.

## Voluntary donations

If you find Skribane useful, you can support its development with a **voluntary donation**. Donations are not required and do not unlock additional features — they are simply a way to help keep the project going.

## Updates

The developer strives to maintain and improve the app, but no guarantee is made regarding the frequency or continuity of updates.

## BYOK model (Bring Your Own Key)

AI costs are paid by the user with their own API keys from [OpenAI](https://platform.openai.com/), [Google Gemini](https://ai.google.dev/), or [Anthropic](https://console.anthropic.com/). You can also use free local models with [LM Studio](https://lmstudio.ai/) or [Ollama](https://ollama.com/) at no cost.

Skribane does not charge for AI usage.

## Privacy

All data is stored locally on your computer. No accounts, no cloud, no telemetry. More details in [FAQ](/en/faq).
